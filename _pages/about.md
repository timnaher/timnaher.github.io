I am a NeuroAI research scientist at [Meta Reality Labs](https://about.meta.com/realitylabs/) in New York, where I develop real-time deep learning models for peripheral neural interface decoding.

Previously, I completed my PhD in Systems Neuroscience at [Radboud University](https://www.ru.nl/en), supervised by [Prof. Pascal Fries](https://www.kyb.tuebingen.mpg.de/person/59524/2549) at the [Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics](https://www.kyb.tuebingen.mpg.de/en) (Tübingen) and the [Ernst Strüngmann Institute](https://www.esi-frankfurt.de/) (Frankfurt). My thesis, *Latent Structure in Brain and Behavior: From Geometric Brain State Decoding to Saccadic Rhythmicity*, combined Riemannian geometry, causal deep learning, and stochastic modeling to decode brain states and characterize neural dynamics across species.

My work sits at the intersection of computational neuroscience, deep learning, and neural engineering. I build real-time models that decode, predict, and interact with biological neural systems -- from EMG-based neuromotor interfaces to brain-state classifiers for clinical diagnostics.

## Research Interests

- Neural decoding & real-time models
- Deep learning for neuroscience (PyTorch, causal CNNs, LSTMs, Transformers)
- Brain-computer interfaces (fNIRS, EEG, EMG)
- Riemannian geometry for neural data
- Saccade dynamics & visual neuroscience
- Sleep, memory & closed-loop neurostimulation
